We were bred from the beginning to be America’s best all-inclusive indoor/outdoor dog care facility offering dog care services including dog grooming, dog daycare, dog boarding, dog training, in-home services, and a pet retail store. Our facilities promote healthy and happy dogs through socialization, physical exercise, mental stimulation, professional grooming, and a holistic retail approach. Whether your dog is running in our play yards while socializing with friends, having a relaxing spa treatment, or finding their favorite food, treat or toy in the retail store, PupHup is your one-stop-shop for all of your pup’s wants and needs.

Boarding

Treat your pup to an all-inclusive STAYcation at PupHup! With all-day play, individualized care, and spacious private suites, boarding with us gives pups that home away from home feeling! Dog boarding includes room, bedding, bowls, a full day of dog daycare and medication administration if needed. Luxury suites and boarding add-ons are also available for those looking to upgrade their dog’s stay even more!

Daycare

PupHup’s goal is to ensure that all of our guests are stimulated both mentally and physically while in our care. Dogs that join us for dog daycare enjoy spacious indoor/outdoor supervised group play areas featuring interactive elements, such as benches, bridges, wading pools, and, of course, our canine handlers. Since not all dogs play alike, we are prepared for many different playgroups that are separated primarily by age and playstyle. We believe that age is more important than size when evaluating compatibility for dog daycare. Pups who participate in daycare at PupHup are more adjusted, less stressed, healthier and happier.

Grooming

PupHup, we have created a relaxing, stress-free, spa-like atmosphere where professional groomers pamper your pup with a wide variety of different services available. PupHup offers full-service grooms, baths, shed-reduction treatment, teeth brushing, nail trims, nail grinds, and more. PupHup uses the same high-quality products you expect in your personal care products, promising to advocate for the animal’s health, safety, welfare, and comfort over all other factors.

Shop

The Retail Store at PupHup features premium foods, supplements, treats, chews and toys. Our careful and detailed selection process ensures that every product we carry will create a happy and healthy pet. Every brand of food we carry ranks a minimum of three stars, and many are limited-ingredient, grain and gluten-free, and easily digested. Whether it be a specific product for puppies, seniors, breeds, special diets, or allergy avoidance, we have what you are looking for.

Training

From puppy class to therapy dog preparation and everything in between, PupHup offers a variety of dog training services. Classes vary from group settings to one on one sessions and take place in our facilities as well as your home. Please contact your local facility for their available options and pricing.
